Yefrari-d yiṭij ɣef teqbaylit, yuli wass.
Atan ldiɣ amiḍan, ilmend n teqbaylit, ulac ayen nniḍen.
Tanemmirt-ik teldiḍ-iyi abrid ɣer tafat twelheḍ-iyi ferḥeɣ aṭas.
Saramaɣ-ak ad tawḍeḍ ɣer wayen akk tebɣiḍ.
Tanemmirt-ik i kečč kan ay izem.
Yemmal wul ɣer ɣur-s.
Yessaram-it yal tikelt ara t-id-yemmekti.
Icreq-d yiṭij, nefsusin wulawen.
I tecbeḥ tmuɣli n win iḥemmlen.
Ffɣen-d yiselmaden ad sutren tilelli i yimeḥbas n rray.
Fɣent-d ula d tiselmadin, d tilawin wwint-d ɣef yiɣil-nsen lufanat.
Agdud i mazal yessutur tilelli , almi i yella deg leḥris.
Tamurt d tamurt-nneɣ nerna aɣunzu ɣef wannay-nneɣ.
Ayen yerzan tamurt; akken qqaren: ihedder umeksa uqbel ara d-yas bab n wayla.
Argaz tameṭṭut nnan-as barkaɣ ddel d temḥeqranit.
Win yebɣan ad yexdem ɣef teqbaylit irebbi mmi-s fell-as.
Akken tzerɛeḍ ara tmegreḍ, ihi mmel-as i mmi-k laṣel-is, anda yedda uẓar ad yeddu.
Sḥasfeɣ aṭas ɣef tmeslayt-nni i ǧǧan warraw-is.
