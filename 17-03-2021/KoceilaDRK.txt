Nefraq am ivawwen ɣef lluḥ
Tikli-inu am tikli n ifiraɛqes
Leḥuɣ s tmara deg uvrid am udarwic
Ḥulfaɣ s tayri n tidet asmi i tewalaɣ
Amzun ulliɣ adrar
Zit uzemmur d ddwa n leqvayel
Allen n mmi-w cvant allen-im
Imi tella lmut i nḥemmel tudert
Ɣur-k ičeḍ uvrid a mmi
Teqqarbed ɣer ɣur-i tenna-id ḥemlaɣ-k
A mmi ɣur-k iḥviven n tikerkas !
Ad tɛiwḍeḍ zwaj ma mmuteɣ ?
Txuṣ-ik tsugint a gma
Taqbaylit d tutlayt-iw
Azrem ɣef uɛebbuḍ i yelleḥu
Lefraq d aṭan ur nḥellu
Rruɣ deg iḍ i tziri
Tamɣart s tmucuha , igerdan ɣer rrif lkanun
Ddunit tebna ɣef tidet d tkerkas
Ul-iw yebɣa-kem , allen-im uggint-iyi
Deg tegrest , yekkat udfel
Aksil yuɣed aɛawdiw amaynut
Yuba yenṭaq yenna-d tidet
Din Ṛebbi ur tissin ula d yiwen
Cwi mazal aṭas n tmeɣriwin
Qimaɣ d yiman-iw qqareɣ-as ma ḍelmaɣ iman-iw
Leɛmer uffiɣ ayen bɣiɣ , d lebɣi iyi-d-yettafen
Ayen i denniɣ d ul-iw it-yebɣan
Taqbaylit ttawiɣt deg iles-iw , amzun d lfuci , sekra n wwin id-yeqarben ad tewtaɣ i yis.
Ajraḍ yebbin idles , ass-a sefqen-as
Ayuz fell-ak imi id-bbiḍ akayaḍ-inek
Tilleli yal ass , azaglu yubbas
Taqbaylit am waman , d nettat i d aẓaṛ n tudert
Sufɣent seg tadart imi ur yelli am nutni !
Tekker temmes deg umkan-agi !
Yenṭaq unelmad yenna A mass-a acuɣer id-yekkat ugeffur ?
Eḍṣiɣ-am-d nwiɣ am kelxaɣ
A yemma aɛzizen fell-i , ur zmireɣ ara akem-aysaɣ
Ugadeɣ lmut asma ad tawi widak aɛzizen ɣef ul
Ass-a yentayi usennan
Anwa iwumi ara ḥkuɣ asma ad egriɣ waḥdi ?
