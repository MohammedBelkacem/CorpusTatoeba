Azekka ad ruḥeɣ ɣer ssinima.
Tsawel-iyi-d tamdakkelt-iw akken an ṛuḥ lwaḥid ɣer ssinima.
Akka cwiya ad ṛuḥeɣ ad d-awiɣ mmi seg uɣerbaz.
Tegneḍ neɣ mazel ?
Ass-a sεiɣ aṭas n ccɣel.
Aṭas n ccɣel i mazal a t-xedmeɣ uqbel ad gneɣ.
Nesεa yiwet n tgellidt ssawalen-as Dihya.
Amek i s-qqaren i umedakkel-ik ?
Tesεiḍ aṭas n yimeddukal neɣ aya ?
Sεiɣ aṭas n yemdukal.
Nwiɣ kem d tamdakelt n Dihya.
Tamurt n leqbayel tezga-d deg tefriqt n ugafa.
Ḥemmleɣ tumucuha n leqbayel.
Ḥemmleɣ učči n leqbayel.
Nessaram tutlayt taqbaylit ad tuɣal am tid i d-yufraren deg umaḍal.
Ttxiḍiɣ taqendurt.
Amek tga teɣmi n tqendurt-im ?
D taqendurt tazeggaɣt.
D taqendurt tawraɣt.
Ass-a Dihya telsa taqendurt-nni tawraɣt i s-xaḍeɣ.
Tecbeḥ tqendurt-im.
Ayɣer ur tebɣiḍ ara ad telseḍ taqendurt?
Ur ttlusuɣ ara taqendurt-a.
Γur-i d taqendurt Iwaḍiyen icebḥen akk gar tqendyar n leqbayel.
Zemreɣ ad εerḍeɣ taqendurt-a ?
Terna fell-i tqendurt-a?
Terna fell-as tqendurt-nni.
Ur semmḍit ara waman agi.
Melmi ay tlul tgellidt Dihya?
Melmi ay temmut tgellidt Dihya?
Qrib azgen n tsaεet nek ttrajuɣ.
Azul fell-awen akken ma tellam !
