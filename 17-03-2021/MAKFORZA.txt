Yenxeẓwaṭ uɛejgur-nni amcum.
D acu-t uɛeẓmum n wagnun?
Henni iman-ik neɣ ad nɣerwaceɣ af yiɣef-ik!
Teḥma tḥenqust-im a tinnat.
Ur ggan ara di temxendeqt, mulac ad texmermerḍ.
Yenḍebbal wul-is ɣef teqbaylit.
Zrurgi af tala arma tuɣal tenneẓẓeblac.
Mer ad teskurfeẓ aɣrum-is, ad as-bruɣ.
Jelweq ifassen-im akken ad ɣendefleɣ udem-im.
Yenheẓgeṭ fell-as am uḍeɛjun.
Beṛka ur sxufur ara am uḥeẓgut.
Teɛceq lalla di uceqbub n uḍellaɛ.
Win yeqḍufren awal-is, ur itteḍraf ul-is.
Kukran waklan ad ččen aɣeḍruf n wuffal.
Ɛenquṣber am yilef yerna ur tettawiḍ aḥenẓul-ik.
Tenxecgar amzun yuɣ-itt uxeẓwiṭ.
Iddu itteddu am uɛejgur ugiddu.
Hlalzen yiɣyal, yenqeẓlaf wuccen.
Yettexbibiṭ am uxerbiṭ, issess am tberwiṭ.
Gdurḥent wallen-is am uqeẓmuḍ ugenduz.
Yeqnuffed wergaz mi iwala taḥenqabut n weɣyul.
Tom d aqelqabu n Layla.
Teḥwecceq tmeṭṭut-nni gar tmerfuftin n wakal.
Iqẓiẓ iga-tt, abuxellaṭ ixelleṣ-itt.
Mastan yesxuẓẓuṭ kan deg Tatoaba.
Mastan yenxeẓwaṭ deg waluḍ n yirkucen.
Anwa-t Mastan-ayini yesqerwicen deg Tatoaba?
Mastan ur yessin ara ad yefruqqec tifyar-is.
Mastan yeḥnuqqeẓ wawal-is am uleqluq.
A Mastan, acimi akka texrubgeṭ taɛeẓluṭ-ik?
