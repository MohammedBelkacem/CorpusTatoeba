Azekka ad t-ẓreɣ ad ssawḍeɣ izen, ma tebɣiḍ.
Nerza ɣer tala n wadda.
Taddart-nneɣ tebḍa ɣef sin yeḥricen, taddart n ufella akked taddart n wadda.
Yecna Yidir deg taddart-nneɣ.
Nexdem tiwizi deg taddart-nneɣ.
Acimi ulac aɣerbaz deg taddart-nwen?
Acimi inselmen rezzun ɣer Mekka ?
Inwa ayen akk yettemcalen d ddheb.
Yettirriq wudem-is.
Aselway n tmurt-nneɣ sersent-id wuffiren.
Sami yekkes akk uṭṭunen-nni n usawal.
Faḍil yeɣra s usawal ɣef ṭnac n yiḍ.
Texsi tkamra n Sami.
Ass-a neɣ azekka mazal imdanen ttemyexbacen.
Amdan-a yegguma ad yeɛyu deg usxerweḍ.
Tilelli n wawal ad nemmet fell-as.
Aqbayli d gma-s is-yefkan tiyta.
Yenna-iyi-d yiwet n lhedra, erfiɣ maci d kra.
Tuddar-nneɣ cebḥent.
Iberdan-nneɣ htutan.
Fkiɣ-as abeqqa.
Yeqqim d aḥedrif meskin.
D acu iɣ-tewwi tsertit-nneɣ?
Bezgen iḍaren-ines seg tikli.
Ikabaren n tsertit bḍan am yibawen ɣef lluḥ.
Acu d iswi n umussu n lḥirak adzayri?
Γunzaneɣ leǧnas meṛṛa.
Nenwa tekfa tegrawla, ziɣ mazal tebda.
D acu d-yenna uneɣlaf n waddal ass-a?
Ḥemmleɣ tiqaqqatin.
