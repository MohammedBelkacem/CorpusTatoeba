Fehmeɣ ayen akken id yenna.
D acu i tebɣiḍ ad d-iniḍ.
Iqqen aɣyul ɣer ttejra.
Mḥend iṛuḥ ɣer uɣerbaz.
Ur ttru ɣef uẓar ma yeqqur, wamma afurk ad yexlef.
Iḥemmel ad yecnu tizlatin n Maṭub Lwennas.
D awḥid i t-id yeǧǧa baba-s.
Luleɣ deg aggur n ɣuct.
Iḥemmel-itt armi yedderwec fell-as.
Taqbaylit d tutlayt ger tutlayin n twacult tameqqrant Tamaziɣt.
Zidane i yellan d amyurar n tarbaɛt n Fransa, laṣel-is d Aqbayli.
Hder ṣṣwab s yiles-ik.
Yiwen ger ungalen i yura Mulud At Mɛemmer s Tefṛansist, isem-is tawrirt yettwattun.
