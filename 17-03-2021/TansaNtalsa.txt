Amek mazal-ik tettaruḍ idlisen ?
Yiwwas tqessar d yiwen uselmad
Taqcict teṭṭef deg wawal-is.
Amkan-is deg uxxam yeqqim d ilem
Setti, teǧǧa-yaɣ tura azal n sin iseggasen-aya
Tiɣmert i deg tettɣimi deg ufrag, mazal nettwali tugna n laxyal-is
Mazal tasga n uxxam tettawi-yaɣ-d ṣṣut-is
Tikwal tettru, ttwaliɣ imeṭṭawen-is mi d-neggin deg wallen-is.
Dayen a mmi, nekk iferr-iw werraɣ, qrib ad yaɣli
Lhemm yeskuɛɛuc, rbaḥ yesluluc
Setti deg wasmi i as-d-cfiɣ d tamɣart.
Ḥemmleɣ aṭas setti, ula d nettat tḥemmel-iyi aṭas, tesbeggin-d afrayen-is ar ɣur-i
Aṭas n tmucuha iyi-d-ssawel, yal tamacahut d tamsirt, yal tafyirt d izen n ddunit
Ar ass-a mazal tugna n wussan-nni deg wallaɣ-iw
Taḥbes temsriffegt deg unafag
Aql-iyi di tmurt n Fransa, di temdint n Marsay.
Wa yesɛa anda ara yeṭṭes, wa ulac.
Tuɣal lqahwa d axxam-nsen.
Yeffeɣ Yennayer, yekcem Furar. Mazal ar tura ur d-yewwit ara ugeffur, tekkaw tmurt.
Tagrest tejebbed alamma d tagara n wagur n Yebrir.
Leḥḥuɣ di tlemmast n temdint.
Kra deg-sen bdan steqsayen-iyi-d amek i ǧǧiɣ tamurt, acu umaynut yellan ?
Qerbeɣ ar ɣur-sen qqimeɣ gar-asen, zzin-iyi-d d agraw.
Yal yiwen deg temzi-s ur yezgil urar.
Aṭas n wuraren i d-wwin yiṛumyen, ama d iselmaden neɣ d imrabḍen iṛumyen akked yiserdasen.
Llan wuraren i tturaren d tirebbuyaɛ ma drus sin yid-sen.
Aselway-nneɣ d amuḍin.
Γef tɣaltin n temẓi-w, yewwet udfel d amellal.
Yekkat ugeffur fell-as.
Llant snat n tudrin di tmurt n Leqbayel.
Ulac tiɣilt i deg ur d-yesṭuṭuc ara uzeɣṭuṭ n usirem.
Amdan d tafekka kan, d tacriḥt n uksum yesbedd wustu d yiɣsan.
Xas ma di temḍellas, yettwali deg-s ifeṭṭiwjen n usirem, d ifeṭṭiwjen d-yesbanayen tella tafat.
Amdan turez-it tuggdi i as-igezzmen abrid akken ur yaẓ ar sdat.
Zik i d-kkreɣ ass-a, kkreɣ-d uqbel ad d-ilal yiṭij.
Gneɣ s uɣbel, uggadeɣ ad iyi-tawi tnafa.
Sliɣ i tmeqwa n ugeffur ad d-ɣellint seg yigenni.
Mazal ur d-tban ara tafat, agu yergel abrid, ur tettɛedday deg-s tmuɣli.
Kkreɣ nudaɣ-d akk tixxamin, tanwalt, ulac d acu ufiɣ.
Mlaleɣ-d Tafat deg ubrid-iw, tafat n ddunit-iw, tin rjiɣ acḥal n yiseggasen.
Tefreḥ yemma asmi i as-nniɣ uffiɣ-d taqcict ara yilin d zzwaǧ-iw
Medleɣ tawwurt deffir-i, suḍeɣ taceɛlalut n tfekkact-nni yersen ɣef ugaṭu.
Azekka-nni ur ttuɣ ara, ruḥeɣ ad d-aɣeɣ arraz.
Ur faqeɣ ara s useggas mi yezri.
Akken zziɣ walaɣ tili d talɣa n tfekka n umdan di lḥiḍ tɛelleq.
Walaɣ lexyal n yijufar n tqendurt yekcem ar tenwalt, ḍefreɣ-t ad waliɣ dacu-t.
Walaɣ yiwet n tewlaft tɛelleq di tesga n tḥanut.
Amen neɣ qqim, d netta i d-uffiɣ da.
Ddurt kamel nutni d anadi, ur uyisen ara.
Yemma-s n teqcict, sin wagguren ur teɛriḍ talqimt.
Tafekka-s tekkaw ala aglim ɣef yiɣes, ulac win ur tɣaḍ ara di taddart.
Ttrun akk yid-s yimezdaɣ maca ur zmiren ara ad as-tt-id-rren.
Nelḥa aṭas, akken newweḍ ar tizi taneggurt n tddart.
Ur t-yessaweḍ ara, iɛedda wi iɛeddan, tensa taluft.
Ulac win yeẓran ma d tidet.
Ḥkiɣ-as-d ayen yeḍran seg wasmi teqqen taxatemt-nni.
Ḥkiɣ-as-d targit-iw tamenzut.
Teṭṭeɛ-iyi-d tuggad, greɣ-tt-id gar yifassen-iw.
Ur bɣiɣ ara ad iyi-truḥeḍ.
Alarmi aṭas ufiɣ-d yiwen n umagrad deg-s yedda-d yisem n tmeṭṭut-nni akked tugna-s.
Fehmeɣ belli iziɣer n tmeṭṭut-nni mazal-it yedder.
Azekka-nni kkreɣ-d zik am yal ass, ruḥeɣ s axeddim-iw.
Ayen akken uggadeɣ ad yeḍru, yeḍra-d.
Nruḥ neṭṭef taxxamt deg usensu.
Ma teffeɣ ayhuh, ma teqqim di texxamt ayhuh, tḥar deg yiman-is.
Akken texdem mačči akken, anda teqim mačči d amkan.
Mi tesla i lḥess ad tefriwes.
Walaɣ talɣa n tfekka tɛellaq di tewwurt n tenwalt.
Fehmeɣ belli dayen tegzem tara n usirem i aɣ-icudden.
Tilelli iqal-itt baba-s, yeggul ar tt-yefk i umenzu tt-id-yewwten, uqbel ad as-d-tawi tacmat.
Ur ifuk ara unebdu imi sliɣ i yisalli ur iyi-nessefreḥ, baba-s yefka-tt i yiwen n waǧǧal.
Ddurt uqbel ad teddu d tislit, terra tilist i wussan-is, ufan-tt di texxamt n walim tɛelleq iman-is.
Ur tt-id-ẓriɣ ara uggadeɣ ad d-ssaliɣ urfan n baba-s d watma-s, ad d-ggriɣ d inzi i tsutwin d-yettnekkaren.
Medleɣ tawwurt n wul-iw, meḍleɣ tayri-w deg-s.
Zgiɣ ttarguɣ yir tirga, ljeṛḥ-iw ur as-ufiɣ ddwa.
Ur ḥemmleɣ ara ad t-waliɣ, serwaleɣ-d allen-iw fell-as.
Zgiɣ dbedbeɣ, dermumẓeɣ, yenɣa-yi ɛawaz.
Tkemmel-iyi tuggdi mi tt-walaɣ uḍan-nni imezwura.
Xas ma tella akkin i lmut, maca yettbaddad-iyi-d lexyal-is yal iḍ di texxamt-iw.
Zrin tura sin n yiseggasen deg wasmi temmut Tlelli, lexyal-is mazal-it yedder yid-i, urjin iyi-yexḍi.
Deg wasmi tgedleɣ, urjin t-walaɣ ibedd-d, urjin sliɣ i lḥess. Ma d tugna-s teṭṭef tasga deg walleɣ-iw, teqqim d asmekti.
Ikfel-iyi-d ɛeggu annect-ilat, serseɣ aqerruy-iw ɣef ṭṭabla.
Bɣiɣ ad ṭṭseɣ.
Ifassen-iw di sin feclen.
Sliɣ-as tettnadi fell-ak.
Kečč, tiṭ-ik tugi ad tt-tẓar.
Waleɣ imɣi-k yettɣar, warreɣ yebda yettibrik.
Yeffeɣ-d wabbu deg yixxamen.
Laḥzen yeɣli-d ɣef tmurt.
Mazal amennuɣ ad ikemmel.
Wehmeɣ d-acu akka iṣaṛen !
Takufit d ukufi ffuncen.
Yesḥallef-d s uɛekkaz, icnef-iyi d acnaf deg wudem.
Nniɣ-as tidet, icennef fell-i.
Ǧǧiɣ aceččuy yegma alarmi d-yeɣli ɣef tuyat-iw.
Deg wasmi d-luleɣ akka.
Uɣaleɣ d lxiḍ, tazmert-iw yečča-tt leɛtab, tehzel.
Kra tekka tegrest ur yi-teffiɣ ara tusut.
Ḥemmlen-iyi ḥesben-iyi am yiwen deg-sen.
Nekk daɣen ḥemmleɣ-ten ḥesbeɣ-ten am twacult-iw.
Ddmeɣ-d amafus-inu ssawleɣ-as i yemma.
Deg ubrid-iw mmugreɣ-d aṭas n tkeṛṛusin ḥebsent.
Iruḥ lḥal, yebda ad d-iɣelli ṭṭlam.
Alarmi aṭas tɛedda-d tmacint tekkes adfel deg ubrid.
Iḍarren-iw gersen.
Inelmaden-iw ferḥen imi walan yekkat udfel.
Ad yergel abrid yettawin ɣer taddart-nneɣ.
D kečč i ifernen axeddim dagi mačči d nekk.
Ma truheḍ, atan ittusemma tejjiḍ amkan-ik.
Ihi aɣbel-agi inek, mačči inu.
Yiwwas deg unebdu, ruḥeɣ ɣer temdint n Iɛeẓẓugen.
Wwiɣ abrid-iw s ukerrus.
Abeḥri n dinna d asemmaḍ.
Mazal-iyi beddeɣ kan akken, ttmuquleɣ-ten iẓri-w yeččur-d d imeṭṭi.
Muqleɣ zelmeḍ, walaɣ yiwen n urgaz isebbay.
Mlalent wallen-iw d wallen-is, yessefrawes-iyi.
Yezwar-d kra s ul-iw, uggadeɣ ad yeḍru kra yid-i.
Sserdeɣ udem-iw.
Igerrez wass.
Abeḥri yeskikiḍ iferriwen n yisekla.
Rwiɣ afenǧal n lqahwa.
Muqleɣ ar seɛɛa, walaɣ-tt iruḥ lḥal.
Abrid yekker, yeččur d ixemjan.
Teqqim sdat lemri.
Ussan n tegrest wezzil-it, iṭij mi d-yecreq din ad d-yeɣli.
Iɛeggalen-is ttraǧun s lḥir talalit n llufan-nsen.
Tamɣart-is teqqim s idis-is, teṭṭef-as afus-is.
Nekk kmumseɣ kan di teɣmert ugadeɣ.
Ur ẓriɣ ara ma d tilawt neɣ d targit.
Yiwen wass, yeṭṭef-iyi lḥal deg yiḍ, ageffur yekkat d azayaḍ.
Ibedd kan, yugi ad yembiwel.
D amɣar, d areqqaq, d aɣezfan n lqedd, iselsa-ines d imellalen.
Ḥulfeɣ am wakken ur yid walan ara.
Ṭṭfeɣ kan imi-w, ssusmeɣ.
Di yal tasga llant tekwatin, uɣent tcemmaɛin.
Akken yezgen yiḍ, tessak-iyi-d tafat d ineqren di teḍwiqin.
Ḥulfeɣ s isebbaḍen-iw d iẓayanen, ččuren d aman.
Beddeɣ ttraǧuɣ, ttxemmimeɣ amek id ixef-is.
Ifassen-iw di leǧyub-iw, aqrab-iw ṭṭfeɣ-t s gar tgecrar-iw, kmumseɣ, senndeɣ ar tgejdit n trisiti.
Yezzi-d ar ɣur-s, iwala tafekka tuli nnig n tfekka n tmeṭṭut-is.
Deg yiḍ mi ara teggen, tettḥulfu s kra ijebbed-as taduli.
Tikwal d asgerweḥ, tikwal niḍen d aẓenẓen n tewwurt.
Udem-is am tin yarfan.
Tawenza tezga tekres.
Asmi wweḍeɣ ar tesnawit, mlaleɣ d kra n yinelmaden iyi-yugaren tamusni.
Ḥemmleɣ aṭas ad sleɣ i yisefra, ḥemmleɣ daɣen ad ɣreɣ tamedyazt d wayen akk icudden ar tsekla n tmaziɣt.
Asmi kecmeɣ ɣer tesdawit, dinna ufiɣ ayen akk yebɣa wul-iw.
