Iqerreḥ-iyi uzagur-iw ass-a, wissen iwacu?
Deg taddart-nneɣ yal aseggas tettili ɣer ɣur-neɣ twizi.
Imucaɣ ixxamen_nsen d aqeḍun .
Yemmut meskin d amengur .
Yeɣli gma ifeddex yiwen ufeddix !
Ass-a ugmeɣ-d abidun n waman.
Ufiɣ-d yaya tgen ɣef ulemsir.
Nwiɣ mačči akka !
Agem-d aman deg tala n wadda.
Taseddaṛt iɣef i nuli, nnig-s ur d-yekki kra .
Nekkni d Iqbayliyen, nessaram timunent i Tmurt Taqbaylit.
Qessiḥ uzekka, iban di tmeddit.
Azul merra fell-awen.
