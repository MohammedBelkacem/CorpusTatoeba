Uɣeɣ-d takeṛṛust.
Ugmeɣ-d aman seg tala.
Skud nedder, tudert-nneɣ i teqbaylit.
Tafriqt n ugafa tesra ɣeṛsum yiwen uwanek amaziɣ.
Seg tirga ar tilawt.
Ass mi d-nsuɣ: tilelli.
D tullist tameẓẓyant, maca anamek-ines meqqeṛ.
Kker fell-ak, d akud ad neddu.
Arrac tturaren deg ufrag.
Yella ɣur-k wuṭṭun-ines?
Qim kan da!
Ass-a ur netturar ara duminu, aceku ɣur-nneɣ inebgiwen.
Send ad tzegreḍ abrid, muqel yeffus zelmeḍ.
